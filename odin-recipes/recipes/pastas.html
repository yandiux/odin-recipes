Basic Homemade Pasta

Description

This is a basic homemade pasta recipe. It's a simple pasta with a classic taste. The recipe is easy to follow and the pasta is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of water

Steps

  • Place the flour on a clean surface and make a well in the center.
  • Crack the eggs into the well and add the salt and water.
  • Using a fork, beat the eggs and gradually incorporate the flour.
  • Knead the dough until smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes.
  • Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  • Roll out the dough and cut it into your desired shape.
  • Cook the pasta in boiling salted water for 2-3 minutes.
