Harvest Salad Recipe

Description

This is a classic harvest salad recipe. It's a simple salad with a classic taste. The recipe is easy to follow and the salad is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of walnuts
  • 1/4 cup of dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup of feta cheese

Steps

  • Wash the mixed greens and cherry tomatoes.
  • Chop the walnuts and dried cranberries.
  • Combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, walnuts, dried cranberries, and feta cheese in a bowl.
  • Toss the salad with your favorite dressing.
