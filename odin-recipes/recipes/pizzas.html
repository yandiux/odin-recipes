Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a classic pepperoni pizza recipe. It's a simple pizza with a classic taste. The recipe is easy to follow and the pizza is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of pepperoni

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  • Spread the tomato sauce on the dough.
  • Add the mozzarella cheese and the pepperoni.
  • Bake the pizza for 15-20 minutes.
